## Overview

DayLog is a privacy-first, productivity tool that helps you track your progress and stay focused on your goals.

## Key Features

- **Habit Tracking**: Track your habits and see your progress over time.
- **Goal Tracking**: Set goals and track your progress towards them.
- **Focus Mode**: Block distractions and stay focused on your tasks.
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Use the Pomodoro technique to stay focused and productive.
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync your calendar and get reminders for your tasks.
- **AI Insights**: Get insights and recommendations for improving your productivity.

## Technologies Used

- **React, Next.js**: For fast, server-rendered React applications.
- **Node.js, Firebase**: For backend development.
